Our Mission & Vision

Guided by faith, empowered by the Holy Spirit, and committed to sharing the Gospel to all nations

Our Mission

To be the light of the world and to go and make disciples of all nations, teaching that Jesus Christ is the only way to salvation through forgiveness of sins.

"You are the light of the world. A town built on a hill cannot be hidden."

We are called to illuminate the darkness with God's truth and love, following the Great Commission to share the transformative message of Jesus Christ with every person, in every nation.

Matthew 5:14, Matthew 28:19-20

Our Vision

To be a global ministry empowered by the Holy Spirit, reaching the Middle East, the Philippines, and the uttermost parts of the earth with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

"But you will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes on you; and you will be my witnesses... to the ends of the earth."

We envision a worldwide movement of believers transformed by the Gospel, equipped by the Holy Spirit, and committed to bringing Christ's message of hope and redemption to every corner of the globe.

Acts 1:8

Our Global Focus Regions

The Middle East

Bringing the Gospel to the birthplace of Christianity, sharing the message of salvation in a region longing for spiritual truth.

The Philippines

Strengthening the faithful and reaching the unreached across the islands with discipleship and church planting initiatives.

Uttermost Parts

Extending our mission to remote and unreached people groups, fulfilling the call to take the Gospel to all nations.

Join Us in Fulfilling the Great Commission

Whether through prayer, giving, or going, you can be part of our mission to share the Gospel with the world. Together, we can bring the light of Christ to all nations.
